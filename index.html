About Me

Hello, I’m Tanvir, a tech enthusiast with a passion for solving complex problems through innovative digital solutions. With extensive experience in software architecture, digital product development, project management and business analysis. I’ve had the privilege of leading diverse projects, from enterprise software serving millions to cutting-edge robotics and IoT initiatives.
My academic foundation in Computer Science, paired with industry certifications, enables me to bridge technical expertise and strategic planning. Recognized for my contributions in technology and leadership, I thrive on driving impactful transformations.
Outside of work, I enjoy mentoring aspiring professionals and engaging in meaningful community activities.
